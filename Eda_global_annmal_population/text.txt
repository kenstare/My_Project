### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Four fundamental patterns emerged:

1. **S-shaped trajectory** — classic logistic growth pattern
2. **Growth rate peaked in 1968** at 2.11% and has fallen continuously since
3. **Absolute annual increase peaked around 1990** (~90 million/year)
4. **Log(Population) shows clear bend downward after 1990** — departure from exponential growth

These observations strongly suggest the world has entered the **upper asymptote** of logistic growth.

### 4. Final Recommended Model: Blended Cubic → Logistic

To combine the strengths of both:

- Use **Cubic Polynomial** up to **2055** (highest empirical accuracy)
- Smooth transition to **Logistic** thereafter (prevents unrealistic divergence)
- Transition window: 2055–2075 (20 years)

**Logistic parameters fitted:**
- Carrying capacity **K = 11.02 billion** (95% CI: ±0.38)
