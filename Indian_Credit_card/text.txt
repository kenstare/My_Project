### 2. What will Built – Full Banking Intelligence Suite

| Module                          | Technology Used                          | Key Output                                                                 |
|---------------------------------|------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Realistic Customer Creation** | Time-gap clustering                      | 12,000+ realistic customers (no random IDs)                               |
| **Fraud Detection**             | Autoencoder + Isolation Forest           | 99.9th percentile detection, ~40–60 high-risk transactions flagged        |
| **Interactive India Map**       | Folium + Geo-coordinates                 | Live fraud & spending hotspots across 12 major cities                      |
| **Customer Segmentation**       | RFM + K-Means                            | Champions • Loyal • At Risk • Sleeping                                     |
| **Churn Prediction**            | Inactivity > 120 days                    | List of customers about to leave                                           |
| **Credit Limit Recommender**    | 3× average transaction                   | Personalized safe limit increase                                           |
| **Customer Lifetime Value**     | 5-year projected revenue                 | Top 10 VIPs worth ₹10–25 Lakh each                                         |
| **Next-Month High-Spender Prediction** | RandomForest (500 trees)          | Top 15 customers with >70% probability of spending >₹5 Lakh next month    |
| **`final_dashboard.py`**        | Streamlit + Plotly + Folium              | One-click live bank dashboard                                              |
| **Production API**              | FastAPI + Swagger UI                     | Real-time fraud, high-spender, CLV, churn, limit endpoints                 |

### 3. Key Business Insights & Recommendations

| Insight                                      | Finding                                          | Action Recommendation                                      |
|---------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Fraud Hotspots**                          | Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru show highest fraud      | Increase real-time monitoring & 2FA in these cities       |
| **High-Value Spenders**                     | Women + Signature/Platinum cards dominate ₹5L+   | Launch “Women Elite” premium card campaign                 |
| **Category Surprise**                       | Bills & Food > Travel (even high-value)          | Partner with utility companies for cashback                |
| **Seasonality**                             | March, Oct, Dec = peak months                    | Run festive & tax-season offers                            |
| **Churn Risk**                              | 18% customers inactive > 120 days                | Immediate win-back campaign (free fees, bonus points)     |
| **Credit Limit Opportunity**                | Top 500 customers eligible for ₹5–15 Lakh limit  | Auto-upgrade + personalized offer                          |
| **CLV Leaders**                             | Top 100 customers worth >₹15 Lakh over 5 years   | VIP concierge, airport lounge access                       |
